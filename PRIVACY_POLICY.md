## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumina ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Lumina Sacred Records journaling application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Lumina, you may provide the following information:

- **Journal Entries:** Text content you write in your journal entries
- **Mood Data:** Emotional state selections (emoji-based mood tracking)
- **Announcements/Notes:** Personal reminders and system notes you create
- **User Preferences:** Theme settings (light/dark mode), timer preferences

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Local Storage Data:** All journal entries and announcements are stored locally in your browser's localStorage
- **Usage Data:** We do NOT collect usage analytics or telemetry

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No Account Information:** Lumina does not require user accounts or authentication
- **No Email or Personal Identifiers:** We do not collect email addresses, names, or contact information
- **No Payment Information:** Lumina is currently free with no payment processing
- **No Location Data:** We do not collect or process location information
- **No Device Identifiers:** We do not collect device IDs or advertising identifiers

---

## 3. How We Store Your Data

### 3.1 Local Storage (Primary Storage)

All your journal entries, announcements, and preferences are stored **locally** in your web browser using the localStorage API.

**What this means:**
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
- Only you have access to your journal entries
- Clearing your browser's localStorage will permanently delete your data
- Your data is NOT backed up to any cloud server by default

**Storage Location:** Browser localStorage at the domain where you access Lumina

### 3.2 Data Encryption

**Current Status:** Data is stored in plain text in localStorage.

**Security Implications:**
- Anyone with access to your browser can view your entries via browser developer tools
- We recommend not using Lumina on shared or public computers
- Consider using browser privacy features and strong device passwords

**Future Plans:** We are working on implementing optional end-to-end encryption for enhanced privacy.

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Groq API (AI Features)

If you use AI-powered features (such as AI reflections on journal entries):

- **Data Sent:** Journal entry text is sent to Groq's API for processing
- **Processing:** AI analysis is performed on Groq's servers
- **Data Retention:** Groq may retain data according to their privacy policy
- **Your Control:** AI features are optional; you can use Lumina without them

**Groq Privacy Policy:** https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

### 4.2 Pollinations AI (Image Generation)

If you use the Vision Portal feature:

- **Data Sent:** Image prompts are sent to Pollinations AI
- **Processing:** Images are generated by Pollinations AI
- **Your Control:** Image generation is optional

**Pollinations AI:** https://pollinations.ai/

### 4.3 No Other Third Parties

We do NOT share your data with:
- Advertising networks
- Data brokers
- Analytics companies
- Social media platforms

## 5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for:

1. **Providing Core Functionality:** Storing and displaying your journal entries
2. **Improving User Experience:** Remembering your preferences (theme, settings)
3. **AI Features (Optional):** Generating reflections and insights when you request them
4. **Data Portability:** Enabling you to export your data

**We do NOT use your data for:**
- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- Training AI models (beyond your immediate session)
- Profiling or behavioral analysis

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
You can view all your data at any time within the application.

### 6.2 Right to Export (Data Portability)
You can export all your journal entries and announcements as a JSON file:
1. Navigate to Sacred Records
2. Click the "Export" button in the left sidebar
3. Your data will download as a JSON file

### 6.3 Right to Delete
You can delete individual entries or all your data:
- **Individual Entry:** Open an entry and click "Delete"
- **All Data:** Clear your browser's localStorage or browsing data

### 6.4 Right to Rectification
You can edit any journal entry at any time by clicking "Edit" on the entry.

### 6.5 Right to Object
You can choose not to use AI features if you prefer not to send data to third-party APIs.

## 7. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA):

### 7.1 Legal Basis for Processing

- **Consent:** By using Lumina, you consent to the processing of your journal entries for the purposes described in this policy
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary to provide the journaling service you requested

### 7.2 Special Category Data

Journal entries may contain sensitive personal data (health, emotions, mental state). Under GDPR Article 9:

- You explicitly consent to processing this sensitive data by using Lumina
- You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data
- We implement appropriate safeguards (local storage, no cloud sync by default)

### 7.3 Data Transfer

- Your data is stored locally on your device
- When using AI features, data may be transferred to Groq (US-based)
- Groq participates in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework

### 7.4 Your GDPR Rights

- Right to access (Article 15)
- Right to rectification (Article 16)
- Right to erasure (Article 17)
- Right to data portability (Article 20)
- Right to object (Article 21)

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us (see Section 10).

## 9. Data Security

### 9.1 Current Security Measures

- **Local Storage:** Data stays on your device
- **No Cloud Sync:** No server-side storage means no server breaches
- **HTTPS:** All API communications use encrypted connections

### 9.2 Limitations

- **No Encryption at Rest:** Data in localStorage is not encrypted
- **Browser Security:** Security depends on your browser and device security
- **No Authentication:** Anyone with access to your browser can access your data

### 9.3 Recommendations

- Use strong passwords on your devices
- Enable full-disk encryption on your computer/phone
- Use private browsing modes on shared devices
- Regularly export backups of your data

### 9.4 Data Breach Notification

Since we do not store your data on servers, traditional data breaches do not apply. However, if we become aware of any security vulnerability:

- We will disclose vulnerabilities transparently
- We will provide guidance on protecting your data
- Contact information will be provided for concerns
